About the Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth largest moon in the Solar System. Its surface is marked by craters, maria, and highlands, holding mysteries and inspiring humans for centuries.

Fascinating Facts

Phases

The Moon has 8 phases, from new to full and back again every 29.5 days.

First Landing

Apollo 11 was the first mission to land humans on the Moon in 1969.

Tidal Effect

The Moon's gravity causes tides on Earth, influencing life and climate.

Exploration

More than 100 spacecraft have been launched to explore the Moon.
